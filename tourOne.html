Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Beijing

Welcome to Beijing! Your tour guide will be waiting for you at either the airport of train station. A private car will transfer you to your hotel.

Day 2: The Great Wall

A visit to Beijing would not be complete without a trip to the Great Wall. Your guide will pick you up from your hotel and take you to the Mutianyu section. Here, you can take a chairlift up to the top to save time and energy. You may chose to take the lift back down or to ride the toboggans. On the wall, you will have free time to walk around and explore.

Day 3: Summer Palace & Boat Ride

After a traditional Chinese breakfast, go see the Summer Palace. It is a beautiful imperial garden divided into three main areas: the lake area, the palace area, and the mountain area. In warmer months, enjoy a boat ride to view all of the palaces around the lake.

Day 4: The Forbidden City

Your guide will first take you to Tian'anmen Square and will give you information about the history and architecture of the area. Then, you will be able to walk to The Forbidden City, the main sight of the day. Your guide will help you to see several carefully selected palaces allowing you to admire some of the famous buildings and famous figures. Enjoy some local Beijing style cuisine with your guide. After a Chinese lunch, explore the old Hutong area to see old houses and shops.

Day 5: Transfer to Shanghai

To transfer to your next destination, make your way to the railway station and ride the high-speed train It will take about 4.5 hours to get from Beijing to Shanghai.

Day 6: The Bund & French Concession

Visit the Bund and learn about the history of Shanghai. See the Oriental Pearl TV tower and ascend the tallest building in Shanghai, Shanghai tower. When visiting the bund, you can take a ferry across the Huangpu river to enjoy the views. You can also visit the Former French Concession Precinct from the 1930s.

Day 7: Yu Garden and Shopping at Nanjing Road

After breakfast go see the Yu Garden, a classical Chinese garden hidden in the busy city. Your guide will share interesting stories about some of the features in the garden. After lunch, explore the prosperous Nanjing Road. It's a good place for shopping and looking for souvineers.

Day 8: Explore Zhujiajiao Water Town

Enjoy a relaxing day in Zhujiajiao Water Town. Take a stroll by the river and forget how close you are to the busy city.

Day 9: Depart from Shanghai

Your personal guide will help you to get to the airport or train station and will assist with check-in.

From $900 per person, based on 2 people and a 4-star hotel
