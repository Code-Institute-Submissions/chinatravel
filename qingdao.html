Qingdao

About

Welcome to the beautuful coastal city of Qingdao! The olympic sailing events of the 2008 Beijing Games were held here as well as the 2013 Yachting World Cup. Qingdao is also the hometown of the famous Tsingtao Beer. The city's construction is a unique combination of European and traditional Chinese styles. Overall, Qingdao is a beautiful city to see seascapes, climb mountains, and visit carnivals. There are many interesting places to visit!

Explore Qingdao

1) Zhanqiao Pier

(Map location: A)

Zhanqior Pier is one of the most famous landmarks in Qingdao and is right beside a beach. Fun fact: This pagoda is the inspiration for the Tsingtao Beer logo!

2) May Fourth Square

(Map location: B)

This square is the symbol of Qingdao. There are great views of the sea here and if you visit at night, you have the chance to see a light show.

3) Jimo Lu Market

(Map location: C)

This indoor market is an interesting "fake market" and well worth the experience. There are bargains to be found and haggling is commonplace. The market is a maze of clothing, jewelry, and all kinds of souvenirs.

4) Qingdao Beer Museum

(Map location: D)

Learn about Tsingdao Beer, how it's made, and taste the different varieties.

5) Olympic Sailing Center

(Map location: E)

All of the water sports during the 2008 Olympics in Beijing were held here. Now, it is a nice waterfront area with piers, a marina, a lighthouse, and a museum. There are several nice restaurants and shops nearby.

1) Laoshan

(Map location: F)

If you love hiking, Laoshan is a must visit! Laoshan has the highest peak (1132.7 meters) that rises by the Chinese coast. This mountain park is located right by the ocean, so it offers some incredible views of the beautiful sea, majestic peaks, and interesting clouds. Legend has it that it is here on Laoshan that Taoism was created. You can still see the ancient Taiqing temple today.

2) Fushan

(Map location: H)

A great way to start the morning in Qingdao is to hike up the mountain, Fushan. If you are up for an early morning hike, the sunrise and view of the city from the top are spectacular!

3) Golden Island Beach/ Huangdao

(Map location: H)

Often called the "Premier Beach in Asia", Golden Island Beach is a must visit for a nice relaxing afternoon.

4) Zhongshan Park

(Map location: I)

If you are visiting Qingdao in the spring, you cannot miss the cherry and tulip blossoms here. There are more than 170 varieties of trees and flowers, many imported from Japan. In summer, you can see the lanterns exhibit. In autumn, there is a spectacular chrysanthemum exhibit. Any time of year you visit, there will be something beautiful and interesting to see!

When tourists think of Qingdao, they think of Tsingdao Beer. However, many people don't know about the local Qingdao foods. The mineral water from Laoshan is a high quality water used in making many beverages. Since Qingdao is located by the coast, the local cuisine is abundant in seafood!

  • Qingdao Cool Vermicelli
  • Seafood Noodles
  • Roast Squid
  • Wonton Soup
  • Chili Sautéed Clams
  • Braised Pig Feet
  • Tsingdao Beer

Where to Stay

You can book your hotel with booking.com. This website is one of our favorite search engines for locating the best deals on hotels. The best areas to stay in Qingdao:

  • Shinan District: This is the most popular neighborhood to stay in Qingdao for sightseeing. It is also the core downtown area with many hotels and restaurants.
  • Shibei: This area is just north of Shinan. It has many good places for shopping and is close to the old part of town.
  • Laoshan District: Laoshan district is named for the famous Laoshan mountain in the area. It is also close by to some beaches and beautiful views.
  • Huangdao: Located just across the bay, Huangdao is the best place to stay if you are planning to visit the beach.

When to Visit

Located on the coast, Qingdao has a clear oceanic climate. The weather is usually quite agreeable, with spring and summer being hot and humid. Evenings are usually a little chilly, so tourists are advised to bring some warm layers.
