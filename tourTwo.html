Tour Highlights

This 10-day suggested itinerary will take you to three cities full of natural wonders-Chengdu, Guilin, and Suzhou. You will see the most extraordinary scenic destinations, feed the famous Giant Pandas, and experience rural China.

  • Feed Giant Pandas
  • Relax on a river cruise of the Li River
  • Experience the charm of Old Suzhou
  • Taste ethnic minority food

This itinerary is only one possibility to spark your imagination. You can customize your trip to suit your interests and budget!

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Chengdu

Welcome to Chengdu! Your tour guide will be waiting for you at either the airport of train station. A private car will transfer you to your hotel.

Day 2: Giant Pandas

Have the once in a lifetime experience of feeding the Giant Pandas! Volunteer to be a panda keeper for the day. You will be able to clean the panda's room, make them yummy cakes, and learn all about panda preservation.

Chengdu Cultural Experience

Today you will enjoy a laid-back day exploring Chengdu in a local park. Stop in at a teahouse and learn the proper way to make tea. Have fun learning how to square dance with the local retirees. In the afternoon, go to see a Sichuan Opera. Meet the actors and actresses and see how they train and make their costumes.

Day 4: Transfer to Guilin

To get from Chengdu to Guilin, take a short flight. Your personal guide for Chengdu can either travel with you or a new local guide based in Guilin can pick you up when you arrive.

Day 5: Li River Cruise

A cruise on the Li River among the karst scenery is a spectacular experience. You will see the unique mountains, bamboo groves, animals cooling off in the river, and more! After your cruise, your guide will take you to an ancient town where you can learn some local crafts.

Day 6: Guilin

Get off the beaten path and do some exploring! Your guide can the Elephant Trunk Hill and the Reed Flute Cave. The guides will tell you the stories and legends of the area. After, they can take you to wander around the local markets.

Day 7: Transfer to Suzhou

There are two good ways to get to Suzhou from Guilin. The fastest option is to catch a flight to Shanghai, then take a short train ride to Suzhou. Suzhou doesn't have its own airport, but it is about 30 minutes away from Shanghai's domestic airport. You can also take a high speed train and get there directly. Whatever option you choose, we will help you to check in and make the proper arrangements.

Day 8: Taihu Lake

Choose one or two of Suzhou's famous gardens to explore in the morning. In the afternoon, visit Taihu Lake and take a boat ride to see the pagodas.

Day 9: Suzhou Gardens

Start the day at the famous Humble Administrator's Garden- Suzhou's largest and most famous garden. After, visit the Suzhou Museum and see ancient ceramics and woodcarvings. Make your way down Pingjiang Road, a famous canal street filled with shops and snack stalls.

Day 10: Depart from Suzhou

Your personal guide will help you to get to the airport or train station and will assist with check-in.
