Guilin

About

The Chinese claim that Guilin has the finest landscape under heaven. There is a famous phrase that means "East or west, Guilin's scenery is best". This beautiful city has inspired centuries of Chinese poems and landscape pantings. There are several towns that make up the area called Guilin: Guilin City, Yangshuo Town, Xingping Old Town, and Longsheng Town. Guilin's main features are karst landforms, a mirror like river, and caves filled with stunning stalagmites. Guilin is also home to many ethnic minorities, so it is a great chance to see unique customs of the local people.

Nature Tour

Explore Guilin

1) Li River

(Map location: A)

Taking a cruise along the Li River, you gently glide past an endless view of spectacular karst mountains shrouded in mist.

2) Yangshuo

(Map location: B)

Yangshuo is a county under the jurisdiction Guilin and is often considered to be the most beautiful part of Guilin. There are so many interesting sights and attractions here. You can take a cruise on the Li River or Yulong River, visit the ancient West Street, see big Banyan trees, and enjoy the unique mountains.

3) Longsheng Rice Terraces

(Map location: C)

hese are the world's most incredible rice terraces. They were built around 650 years ago and cover several villages near the city of Guilin. The landscape changes colors due to harvesting, so the best time to visit is May - October when they are a lush green color.

4) Elephant Trunk Hill

(Map location: D)

Elephant Trunk Hill (Xiangbishan) is an iconic spot in Guilin. This famous hill resembles an elephant drinking water from the river.

1) Huashan Rock Art

(Map location: E)

Huashan Rock Art Cultural Landscape was listed as a World Heritage Site in 2016. It is an extensive assembly of historical rock art painted on cliffs several hundred years ago. The cliff is about 221 meters (725 feet) long and 140 meters (459 feet) high. They have 1,900 images arranged around the cliff and many of them are unsolved puzzles.

2) Reed Flute Cave

(Map location: F)

The cave got its name from the reeds that grow just outside the cave, that people use to make flutes. The cave has many rock formations, stalactites, and stone pillars that are lit up with many colorful lights.

3) Zhengyang Night Market

(Map location: I)

Night markets are an interesting cultural experience. On the street, you can find many local food, drinks, and souvenirs.

Traditional Guilin cuisine is usually a combination of Hunanese and Cantonese flavors. Many foods have a sour or slightly spicy taste. Stir-frying and steaming are the most common cooking methods of Guilin cuisine.

  • Lipu Taro and Pork Loaf

When to Visit

The Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, where Guilin is located has a subtropical climate. Temperatures in winter are mild and rarely go below freezing. The best time to visit Guilin are March-May and September-November when the weather is mild and the days are sunny.
