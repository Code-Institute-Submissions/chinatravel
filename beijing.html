Beijing

About

Welcome to Beijing! Beijing is the capital city of China and is also one of the buisiest cities in the world. Home to many classic and modern chinese sights, Beijing is one of the most popular cities to visit in China. On a tour to Beijing, you will be able to see the famous Forbidden City, Summer Palace, Tian'anmen Square, and even take a day trip to see the Great Wall of China. Join one of our tours today!

When to Visit

Beijing is a wonderful city to visit most times of the year. However, the winter can be a difficult time to visit with temperatures around the freezing point. To avoid large crowds and busy travel days, plan your trip carefully and not at the time of China's major holidays: Chinese New Year (Late Winter/Early spring) and National Week (The first week in October.)
