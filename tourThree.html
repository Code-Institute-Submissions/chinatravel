Tour Highlights

If you want to learn about the history of China and see some of the most famous cultural sights in the country, this 10-day tour is for you!

  • See some of the old and famous gardens of Suzhou
  • Explore the Forbidden City
  • Try out a handpicked cultural activity

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Shanghai

Welcome to Shanghai! Your tour guide will be waiting for you at either the airport of train station. A private car will transfer you to your hotel.

Day 2: The Bund & French Concession

Visit the Bund and learn about the history of Shanghai. See the Oriental Pearl TV tower and ascend the tallest building in Shanghai, Shanghai tower. When visiting the bund, you can take a ferry across the Huangpu river to enjoy the views. You can also visit the Former French Concession Precinct from the 1930s.

Day 3: Yu Garden and Shopping at Nanjing Road

After a traditional Chinese breakfast, go see the Summer Palace. It is a beautiful imperial garden divided into three main areas: the lake area, the palace area, and the mountain area. In warmer months, enjoy a boat ride to view all of the palaces around the lake.

Day 4: Day Trip to Suzhou

Suzhou is just half an hour away from Shanghai by train. Your guide will take you to the famous Humble Administrator's Garden- Suzhou's largest and most famous garden. After, visit the Suzhou Museum and see ancient ceramics and woodcarvings. Make your way down Pingjiang Road, a famous canal street filled with shops and snack stalls. Return to Shanghai in the evening.

Day 5: Transfer to Qingdao

To transfer to your next destination, make your way to the railway station and ride the high-speed train It will take about 4 hours to get from Shanghai to Qingdao. You can also choose to take a plane to Qingdao. There are usually several flights each day and take 1 1/2 hours.

Day 6: Qingdao

Choose to visit the Beach ar Huangdao or climb Laoshan Mountain. Whatever place you choose to visit, experience the beauty and hear stories about the local area.

Day 7: Afternoon transfer to Beijing

In the morning, visit Zhanqiao Pier and Jimo Lu market. After lunch, go to the railway station to catch a highspeed train to Beijing.

Day 8: Ancient Beijing

Go see The Forbidden City Tian'anmen Square. Your guide will help you to see several carefully selected palaces allowing you to admire some of the famous buildings and famous figures. They will also be able to share interesting stories about the area. After a Chinese lunch, explore the old Hutong area to see old houses and shops.

Day 9: The Great Wall

A visit to Beijing would not be complete without a trip to the Great Wall. Your guide will pick you up from your hotel and take you to the Mutianyu section. Here, you can take a chairlift up to the top to save time and energy. You may chose to take the lift back down or to ride the toboggans. On the wall, you will have free time to walk around and explore.

Day 10: Depart from Beijing

Your personal guide will help you to get to the airport or train station and will assist with check-in.
