Shanghai

About

Welcome to China's largest city, Shanghai. Shanghai offers many sightseeing destinations for any kind of traveler. This city has many famous gardens and museums as well as innovative architecture. Some visitors come to Shanghai for the food, as Shanghai cuisine is considered to be one of China's top cuisines.

Where to Stay

You can book your hotel with booking.com. This website is one of our favorite search engines for locating the best deals on hotels. The best areas to stay in Shanghai:

  • The Bund/ People's Square: If you want to stay in "downtown" Shanghai and in the middle of the action, this is the best place to stay.
  • Pudong: The high end, luxury area of Shanghai with high rises and skyscrapers
  • French Concession: A cultural experience
  • Jing'an: A newer area that has many international convieniences and shops
